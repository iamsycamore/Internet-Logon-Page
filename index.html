IT Security Tips
1. Ensure you have a Licensed Antivirus software installed on your computer
2. Ensure your antivirus software is updated at least once in a week and a full scanning of your computer is done at least twice in a month
3. Avoid downloading from an unsecured sites
4. Desist from giving out your hotspot password to other users
5. Ensure your computer firewall is on and avoid using others USB drive on your computer
6. Upgrade your computer Operating System to a current Operating System
